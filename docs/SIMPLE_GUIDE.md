## 1. What Is This?

### Think of it like Siri or Alexa, but...

- **You control it**: Your data stays on your computer
- **You can talk or type**: Works both ways
- **It's helpful**: Gets weather, finds restaurants, books Uber
- **It learns about you**: Remembers your preferences (with your permission)
- **It's free**: No subscription needed (just need internet)

### What makes it special?

- 🎨 **Looks good**: 5 different color themes
- ♿ **Easy to use**: Works for people with disabilities
- 🔒 **Private**: Your personal info stays on YOUR device
- 🌍 **Location-smart**: Knows where you are and helps accordingly
- 📱 **Works everywhere**: Phone, tablet, or computer

## 3. How to Use It

### Two Ways to Talk to It

#### Way 1: Push-to-Talk (Like a Walkie-Talkie)

1. **Hold** the **Space bar** on your keyboard
2. **Speak** your question
3. **Release** the Space bar
4. **Wait** for the answer

**Example:**
```
[Hold Space] → "What's the weather today?" → [Release Space]
```

---

#### Way 2: Click and Talk

1. **Click** the microphone button 🎤
2. **Speak** your question
3. **Wait** (it stops listening automatically after you're done)
4. Get your answer!

**Example:**
```
[Click 🎤] → "Find pizza places near me" → [Auto-stops] → [Answer]
```

---

#### Way 3: Just Type It

Don't want to talk? That's fine!

1. **Click** in the text box at the bottom
2. **Type** your question
3. **Press Enter** or click the send button ↑
4. Get your answer!

**Example:**
```
Type: "Book an Uber to the mall"
Press Enter
```

## 5. Troubleshooting

### "It's not listening!"

**Check:**
1. ✅ Did you allow microphone access?
   - Your browser will ask when you first try to talk
   - Click "Allow"

2. ✅ Is your microphone working?
   - Try in another app
   - Check your computer's sound settings

3. ✅ Are you holding Space bar (push-to-talk)?
   - Or did you click the microphone button?

**Still not working?**
- Just type instead! Use the text box

---

### "It says 'Not connected'"

**This means:**
- The app can't talk to the brain (server)

**Fix it:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Refresh the page (F5 key)
3. If that doesn't work, close and reopen the browser

---

### "My profile disappeared!"

**This means:**
- Your browser cleared its memory (cookies/local storage)

**Fix it:**
- Just set up your profile again (takes 1 minute)
- It will remember next time!

**To prevent this:**
- Don't clear your browser data
- Or only clear specific sites

---

### "Uber link doesn't work"

**Check:**
1. ✅ Did you add your address in profile? (for computer)
2. ✅ Did you allow location access? (for phone)
3. ✅ Is the destination clear?
   - ❌ "Take me there" → Too vague
   - ✅ "Take me to the mall" → Clear!

**Try:**
- Be specific: "Book Uber to 123 Main Street, New York"

## 6. Privacy & Safety

### What Information Do We Store?

**On Your Device Only:**
- ✅ Your name
- ✅ Your home address (if you provided)
- ✅ Your preferences (favorite cuisines, common destinations)

**Where?**
- In your browser's memory (called "localStorage")
- **NOT** on our servers
- **NOT** in the cloud

**This means:**
- Different browsers = different profiles
- Clear browser data = profile is gone
- Switch computers = need to set up again

---

### What Gets Sent When You Talk?

**We send:**
- Your question/message
- Your profile info (so we can personalize)
- Your device type (phone or computer)

**We DON'T send:**
- Your location history
- Your conversation history
- Any info you didn't share

**To the AI:**
- Your message goes to OpenAI or OpenRouter
- They process it and send back an answer
- They don't store your conversations (for this app)

---

### Can I Delete Everything?

**Yes! Easy:**

1. Click **"Edit Profile"**
2. Scroll to bottom → **"Danger Zone"**
3. Click **"Reset All Data"**
4. Confirm **"Yes, Reset Everything"**

**What happens:**
- All your profile data is deleted
- Like starting fresh!
- Next time you open, you'll see the welcome screen again

---

### Tips for Privacy

1. **Don't share sensitive info**
   - Don't say credit card numbers
   - Don't say passwords
   - Don't share private medical info

2. **Use on personal devices**
   - Not recommended on shared/public computers
   - Your profile stays in that browser

3. **Clear when done (public computers)**
   - Go to "Edit Profile"
   - Click "Reset All Data"

## 8. Common Questions

### "Do I need to pay?"

**No!** The app is free.

**But you need:**
- Internet connection
- A device (phone, tablet, computer)
- A web browser

---

### "Does it work offline?"

**No**, you need internet because:
- It talks to AI brain (online)
- It gets weather data (online)
- It searches restaurants (online)

---

### "Can multiple people use it?"

**Not at the same time** on one browser.

**But:**
- Each browser = separate profile
- Mom's profile on Chrome
- Dad's profile on Firefox
- Sister's profile on her phone

**Or:**
- Just reset and re-setup when switching users

---

### "Is it always listening?"

**No!** Only when:
- You hold Space bar, OR
- You click microphone button

**It stops:**
- When you release Space bar
- After 3 seconds of silence (auto-mode)
- When you click stop

**Red dot = listening**
**No red dot = NOT listening**

---

### "What if I make a mistake?"

**No problem!**

- Just ask again
- Rephrase your question
- The assistant won't remember your mistakes

**Example:**
```
You: "Weatherr todday" (typo)
Assistant: [confused]

You: "What's the weather today?"
Assistant: "Currently 72°F and sunny..."
```
